Ariadne

Ariadne is a humanoid animatronic, inspired by the Greek mythology character linked to the labyrinth of the Minotaur. The project seamlessly combines art with engineering. Initial mechanisms were thoroughly designed with CAD, ensuring functionality and reliability. Concurrently, an emphasis was placed on realism, with authentic face and body features carefully sculpted in a dedicated design software. Transitioning from design to reality, 3D printing played a crucial role. Both the mechanical parts and the specialized molds were materialized through this process, embodying the intersection of innovation and accuracy. Durability and authenticity were prioritized by utilizing theme park grade silicone for the skin, aligning with industry standards for realism. Attention to detail is evident in the individual eyebrow and hair strands, manually punched into the silicone, complementing the overall appearance. The dress, tailored in-house, aligns with the character's historical and mythological context, enhancing the authenticity. The programming, was accomplished using specialized software, allowing for coordinated and smooth movements. The well-integrated technology enables Ariadne to embody the essence of the mythological character, creating a remarkable and engaging presence. Meet Ariadne — a tribute to the convergence of mythology, technology, and creative design, standing as a testament to the application of modern robotic elements with a flair of artistic creativity.

Telemachus

Telemachus Introducing "Telemachus", our latest foray into the world of animatronics. Telemachus is a humanoid animatronic project currently under development. This model shifts from full electrical operation to pneumatic actuation, utilizing pneumatic cylinders for movement, aiming for a stronger and more durable performance. The body frame will be constructed using metal, focusing on long-lasting endurance. This will allow Telemachus to operate efficiently in various environments and withstand long hours of operation without compromising its structural integrity. In terms of appearance, Telemachus will maintain a high standard of realism. The use of theme park grade silicone for the skin and manually punched hair and eyebrows will contribute to a convincingly lifelike presentation. A significant focus will placed on improving the control system and overall body frame of Telemachus. The goal is to enhance the operation quality, allowing for smoother and more refined movement animations. These improvements are aimed at ensuring that Telemachus can operate seamlessly and effectively across different conditions and settings, marking a step forward in the project’s ongoing development. Meet Telemachus - a practical blend of robust engineering, art and mythological inspiration for enhanced performance in diverse environments.
