Ariadne

Ariadne represents a fusion of art and engineering, taking form as a humanoid animatronic inspired by the Greek mythology figure associated with the Minotaur's labyrinth. Utilizing CAD, the project ensures precise design and reliable functionality. Simultaneously, a focus on realism led to the meticulous sculpting of facial and body features using specialized software. The transition from concept to tangible form leaned heavily on 3D printing. This technology was pivotal in materializing both mechanical components and molds. We adopted industry-standard theme park grade silicone for the skin to strike a balance between durability and authenticity. Subtle details like individual eyebrow and hair strands, hand-punched into the silicone, elevate Ariadne's appearance. Her attire, crafted in-house, resonates with the historical and mythological backdrop of the character, bolstering its authenticity. Programming, achieved with advanced software, ensures Ariadne moves with coordinated fluidity. This harmonized use of technology captures the essence of the myth, rendering Ariadne not just an animatronic but a compelling representation of its mythological counterpart. Meet Ariadne - a symbol of the blend between mythology, technology, and design, underscoring the potential of modern robotics and art in storytelling.

Telemachus

Introducing "Telemachus", our latest foray into the world of animatronics. Telemachus is a humanoid animatronic project currently under development. This model shifts from full electrical operation to pneumatic actuation, utilizing pneumatic cylinders for movement, aiming for a stronger and more durable performance. The body frame will be constructed using metal, focusing on long-lasting endurance. This will allow Telemachus to operate efficiently in various environments and withstand long hours of operation without compromising its structural integrity. In terms of appearance, Telemachus will maintain a high standard of realism. The use of theme park grade silicone for the skin and manually punched hair and eyebrows will contribute to a convincingly lifelike presentation. A significant focus will be placed on improving the control system and overall body frame of Telemachus. The goal is to enhance the operation quality, allowing for smoother and more refined movement animations.
